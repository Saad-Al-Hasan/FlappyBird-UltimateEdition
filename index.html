How To Play

🎯 Goal

Guide your bird through the gaps between pipes without crashing. Each pipe you pass earns 1 point.

🕹️ Controls

  • Click / Tap / Spacebar — flap upward, fighting gravity.
  • P — pause / resume the game.
  • Release the flap and gravity pulls the bird back down.

💰 Coins

Yellow coins float near the pipes during a run. Collect them to earn currency, spendable in the Shop on new birds and themes.

⚡ Power-ups

  • Shield — absorbs one collision so you survive a crash.
  • Slow Motion — halves pipe speed for a few seconds.
  • Double Score — doubles points earned while active.
  • Magnet — automatically pulls nearby coins toward you.

Power-ups appear as glowing icons drifting through pipe gaps — fly into one to activate it.

🐦 Birds

Each bird has a unique feel: some fall faster, some glide longer. Unlock new birds with coins earned in-game.

🎨 Themes

Day, Night, Space and Underwater each reskin the whole game — sky, pipes, and background animation.

🏆 Achievements & Stats

Score milestones unlock achievements automatically. Check the Stats page to track your best run, total games, average score, and total time played.

📶 Difficulty

Easy gives wide, slow-moving gaps. Hard gives narrow, fast-moving gaps for experts. The game gets faster as your score increases, so stay focused!
